Listen: the single

French Touch to the core. I write a whole piece of music from scratch, chop it up, and drop it back in as the track's main sample. That cut-and-rebuild is where the groove comes from. The track stands on its own and opens the door to NOVALIS: music and universe are one project, not a single with a story attached. 120 BPM, 2:40, with my own vocal woven in.

About Foxako

The Project

Foxako comes from French-speaking Switzerland. I developed the first chapter on my own: music, narrative direction and 3D animation. For the chapters to come it becomes a duo, with illustrator Keynok shaping the visual world. Sound and image are treated as one: sketches become characters, samples become emotions, and every release is a complete work, not a single with artwork bolted on.

About NOVALIS

The World

NOVALIS is a sci-fi saga in five arcs. It deliberately opens at the very end, planting a mystery from the first frame, then lets each new release rewind through time and answer it, piece by piece. Built slowly, with no rush.
